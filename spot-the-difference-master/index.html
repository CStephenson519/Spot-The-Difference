How to Play.

In this "photo-match" style game, use your mouse to point out differences between two side-by-side picture featuring the New MINI 5 door.

Scoring Points.

  • Score points by clicking all the photo difference for each level.
  • Score 10 points for every correct answer between 0-10 seconds
  • Score 5 points for every correct answer between 11-20 seconds.
  • Score 3 points for every correct answer between 21-30 seconds
  • Score 1 point for every correct answer after 31 seconds.

Losing points.

  • Lose 1 point for every incorrect answer.
